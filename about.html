About TrustCalc

TrustCalc was founded with a simple mission: to provide the most accurate, transparent, and easy-to-use financial tools for residents of the USA and UK. In a world of complex financial products, we believe that clarity should be free and accessible to everyone.

Our Core Principles

  • Bank-Grade Accuracy: Our algorithms are based on real-world financial standards, including standard amortization formulas used by major lenders in the US and UK.
  • 100% Privacy: We never store your data. All calculations happen locally in your browser, ensuring your financial information stays with you.
  • No Hidden Agendas: We are not a lender. Our tools are designed to provide unbiased estimates to help you make informed decisions.

How Our Tools Work

Each calculator on our site is custom-built to reflect the specific financial landscape of the region selected. For example:

  • Mortgages: Our US mode accounts for PITI (Principal, Interest, Taxes, and Insurance), while our UK mode focuses on standard repayment structures and LTV (Loan-to-Value) tiers.
  • Inflation: We use historical CPI (Consumer Price Index) data from the Bureau of Labor Statistics (USA) and the Office for National Statistics (UK) to provide realistic projections.
  • Debt Management: Our strategies (Avalanche and Snowball) are based on proven financial methodologies for debt reduction.

Who Built This?

TrustCalc is maintained by a team of finance enthusiasts and software engineers dedicated to financial literacy. We believe that by providing high-quality tools and educational guides, we can help people navigate the path to financial freedom.

Important Disclaimer

While we strive for 100% accuracy, the results provided by our calculators are estimations only. Interest rates, tax laws, and lender policies are subject to change and vary by individual circumstances. We strongly recommend consulting with a certified financial planner or tax professional before making significant financial decisions.
